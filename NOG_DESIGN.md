# 🚀 NOG - Voxel Universe Explorer

**The Original nog_game.c Design Document**

---

## 🎮 What is NOG?

NOG is a massive multiplayer universe explorer game with:

- **Multi-galactic scale** - 100x100x100 grid, 1 million stars
- **100% destructible environments**
- **MMO environment** - Citizens, Armies, Factions
- **Real-time combat** - Space and planetary

---

## 👤 NOG - The Protagonist

**NOG has 3 forms:**

### 1. ROCK (Most Common)
- Like Apollo 18 rock creatures
- Can absorb DNA, knowledge, tech from beings it contacts
- Feeding takes 30-60 seconds of contact
- When broken, parts connect back to the MASS
- Rocks in space mistaken for asteroids

### 2. CRYSTAL (Rare)
- No movement, enormous power for spacecraft
- Can take the form of last being that touched it (24 hours)
- Under laser examination, triggers elements to activate

### 3. GLOB (Organic Globule) ⭐ THE JOKE!
- **"Nog Nog an Organig Globule"** ⭐
- Moves very fast
- Consumes all matter at molecular level to grow
- Resistant to most energy weapons
- Weakness: COLD - slows to gel state
- Can break into small forms and reform
- Can clone itself (1000 clones!)

## 🎖️ Army vs Citizen

### Army
- Hoist flag for army
- Guild Master controls rules
- Research tech tree
- Build structures and bases
- Command troops and ships
- **Rank progression**: Novice → Experienced → Lieutenant → Sergeant → Captain → Colonel → Commander → General → Admiral

### Citizen
- Join guild, profession-based
- Access VR & RL mall
- Trade, buy equipment
- Can pilot ships
